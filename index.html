◈ THE CONCEPT
The Premise
90% of your trades go the wrong way. This journal flips your direction automatically and tracks what happens if you trade the opposite.
Your Trade vs Counter Trade
Your Trade = what your gut said.
Counter Trade = the automatic flip.
WIN / LOSS
Result is based on the counter trade, not yours. Green = counter won. Red = counter lost (your gut was right).
